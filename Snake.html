Instructions

You are the blue square, and you have to get to the green square to get to the next levels. use the arrow keys or swipe the screen (if mobile) to move your snake. The red squares are fruit, and for everyone that you pick up, you will get a bonus point and grow in length! They appear in random positions though, so you can not always get them. Once you are larger you cannot move directly backwards though! Use the yellow walls to bounce off of and get into position to get to the green portal square.
